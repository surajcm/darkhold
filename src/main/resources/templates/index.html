Features

Everything you need to engage

Powerful tools to create, host, and analyze interactive quiz sessions.

Real-Time Play

WebSocket-powered live gameplay with instant question delivery and real-time score updates across all connected devices.

Team Mode

Split players into teams automatically or manually. Collaborate on answers and compete as a group for added engagement.

Excel Import

Upload your questions from a spreadsheet in seconds. No manual entry needed - just drag, drop, and go live.

Live Analytics

Track participation, accuracy, and performance in real time. Review detailed reports after every session.

Dark Mode

Full dark theme support with automatic OS detection. Easy on the eyes for late-night study sessions and events.

Self-Hosted

Own your data completely. Deploy on your own infrastructure with Docker in minutes. No vendor lock-in, ever.
